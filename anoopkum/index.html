ABOUT

Anoop Kumar is a Senior Computer Scientist and Research Scientist at USC Information Sciences Institute.

He received his Bachelor of Science degree from IIT Kharagpur, India and his Master’s and Ph.D. from Tufts University in computer science. His Thesis focused on Augmented Training Methods for Hidden Markov Models for the Detection of Remote Protein.

Dr. Kumar is a Co-Founder and Technical Director of Lokvani, Inc., MA, member of Association of Computing Machinery, Vice- President, IIT Society in New England (Jan 2001 – November 2004), President & Co-Founder, Indian Society At Tufts, Tufts University (May 99 – May 2000), President & Co-Founder, Technology Chess Association, IIT-Kharagpur
