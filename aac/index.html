AAC

In this project we are creating linked data for 14 American art museums as part of the American Art Collaborative (http://americanartcollaborative.org/). The project will focus on linking the people and locations referenced in the metadata from each of the museums. In order to generate high-quality links, we will develop a crowd-sourced linking tool, which will allow the staff from the 14 museums to quickly and easily validate the automatically generated links.
