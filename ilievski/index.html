ABOUT

Filip Ilievski : Dr. Filip Ilievski is a Computer Scientist in the Center on Knowledge Graphs within the Information Sciences Institute (ISI) at the USC Viterbi School of Engineering. Prior to joining ISI, he obtained a Master degree in Computer Science and a Ph.D. in Natural Language Processing from the Vrije Universiteit (VU) in Amsterdam, under supervision of professors Frank van Harmelen and Piek Vossen. His dissertation research explored how systematic and extensive use of knowledge can help machines to deal with the ‘long-tail’ (knowledge scarcity and ambiguity) of human communication, thus integrating ideas from Information Extraction, Knowledge Graphs, and Machine Learning. His dissertation is being published as a book in the Studies in the Semantic Web series. At ISI, Dr. Ilievski primarily aims to represent and integrate commonsense knowledge graphs, as well as reason over them in order to answer questions in a natural language form.
