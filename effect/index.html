EFFECT

The goal of the EFFECT (Effectively Forecasting Evolving Cyber Threats) project is to accurately forecast emerging cyber threats by integrating information from a variety of novel sensors within a robust inference framework. The project brings together a diverse team of experts to address a number of technical and scientific challenges, including:

1) Finding traces of early planning activity in the face of threat actors obfuscating them using anonymized access to dark forums; 2) Dealing with multimodal data that includes unstructured natural language text, structured data, and public network traffic data such as Domain Name Service (DNS) logs; 3) dealing with large volumes of heterogeneous data within a real-time forecasting framework; and 4) generating robust warnings that integrate information from diverse forecasts.
